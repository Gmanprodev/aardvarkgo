About AardvarkGo

At AardvarkGo, we understand the recruitment industry inside and out. Our platform was born from the collective experience of seasoned recruiters who recognised a critical gap in the market and set out to fill it.

Our Roots

AardvarkGo was created by recruiters, for recruiters. Our founding team brings decades of experience from the front lines of agency recruitment. This deep industry knowledge has been the cornerstone of our approach, ensuring that every feature and function of AardvarkGo is tailored to address the real-world challenges faced by recruitment professionals.

Tech Meets Recruitment Expertise

What sets us apart is our unique blend of recruitment and technical expertise. Our internal development team consists of professionals who have walked in your shoes – they've worked in agency recruitment before transitioning to software development. This dual perspective allows us to create solutions that are not just technically sound, but also intuitively aligned with the needs of recruiters.

Empowering Recruiters with Data-Driven Insights

AardvarkGo's flagship feature is our sophisticated candidate tracking system. We monitor your candidates monthly, providing you with timely notifications if any of them secure positions at companies where you've previously introduced their CV. But we go beyond just alerting you to potential missed fees. Our system offers a depth of knowledge and power that can transform your recruitment practice:

  • Process Optimization: By highlighting patterns in candidate placements, we help you identify and strengthen weak spots in your recruitment processes.
  • Client Relationship Management: Our insights can inform more strategic discussions with clients, potentially leading to improved terms of business.
  • Internal Efficiency: Discover areas where your internal processes may be falling short, allowing you to implement targeted improvements.

At AardvarkGo, we believe that information is power. Our goal is to equip you with the data and insights you need to not just recover missed fees, but to elevate your entire recruitment operation to new heights of efficiency and success.
